### **SLIDE 6: The Solution - DANAYA Platform**

**What I've Built (So Far):**

**Core Features:**
1. **Hospital Registry** - 15+ facilities mapped
2. **Secure Authentication** - Hospital-aware login
3. **Patient Management** - Digital medical records
4. **Role-Based Access** - 5 medical roles
5. **Future: Telemedicine** - Connect rural to urban

**Technical Architecture:**
- Microservices (like Japanese modular design)
- Zero-trust security (never trust, always verify)
- Offline-capable (for intermittent electricity)
- Low bandwidth optimized (2G/3G networks)

**Why Zero-Trust?**
- Inspired by Japan's cybersecurity excellence
- Protect patient privacy (sacred trust)
- Prevent unauthorized access
- Meet international standards

**Why Microservices?**
- Easy to maintain (kaizen approach)
- Can update one part without breaking others
- Scale piece by piece
- Like modular Japanese manufacturing
